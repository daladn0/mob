What does “locked” mean?

You can still log into the account normally, but you are prevented from voting, posting, editing or deleting existing content, moderating, sending private messages (except responses to official notifications from the admins), changing account settings, and deactivating the account. This is to prevent your account from potential misuse or destruction of your content.

Why did you lock my account?

Reddit is constantly working to keep your accounts safe and secure. Our security teams monitor many sources looking for potential threats. We also understand that frequent travel and VPN use are common and these factors are taken into consideration.

How do I unlock my account?

All you need to do is reset your password. Make sure to enter both your username and the email address registered to that username in the fields.

Why can’t I change my email address on a locked account?

Email addresses cannot be changed while an account is locked to prevent any potential unauthorized user from changing the address to one under their control, preventing the original account owner from regaining access.
